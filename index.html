About

Defense R&D executive with 20+ years leading national-scale Electronic Warfare (EW) programs at TÜBİTAK · BİLGEM · İLTAREN, Türkiye's national electronic warfare research institute.

Leading a 300+ engineering organization, defining and executing complex EW system architectures for aerial, land, and naval platforms across EO/IR and RF domains — from concept development through qualification, acceptance, and operational deployment. Multiple programs have successfully transitioned into serial production.

Co-author of 50+ peer-reviewed publications (15 journals, 35+ conferences, 350+ citations, h-index: 13) in infrared sensor-based target detection and tracking.
