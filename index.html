About Hakkımda

Tayfun Aytaç is a defense R&D executive specializing in electronic warfare, AI-enabled sensing, and mission-critical systems. He leads TÜBİTAK BİLGEM İLTAREN, with 20+ years of experience spanning national EW systems, multidisciplinary R&D organizations, academic research, and strategic technology leadership.

Tayfun Aytaç; elektronik harp, yapay zekâ destekli algılama ve görev kritik sistemler alanlarında uzmanlaşmış bir savunma Ar-Ge yöneticisidir.
